Chocolate Sales Analysis
Overview

This project explores chocolate sales data to uncover revenue patterns, product performance, and regional sales trends.

The objective is to analyse business performance, identify high-performing products, and generate actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

Business Questions

Which products generate the highest revenue?

What regions contribute the most to total sales?

Are there seasonal or monthly sales patterns?

Which customer segments drive profitability?

How can sales performance be improved?

Key Insights

A small number of premium chocolate products drive a significant portion of total revenue.

Certain regions consistently outperform others in sales volume.

Sales show seasonal peaks during holiday periods.

Bulk purchase segments contribute higher average transaction value.
